Rebirth

The game is a sci fi hack and slash that takes place in a 3D environment. The player is a cybernetic test subject that was made in a lab. A sudden error caused by an unknown person breaks the player out of its containment capsule. The player doesn’t know where or what he is, except for the urge to escape. The game takes the player through interior environments of a facility. The player uses combo attacks to take down various enemies.
